About QIS College

Excellence in Education Since 1998

College Introduction

QIS College of Engineering and Technology was established in 1998 with a vision to provide quality technical education. Located in Hyderabad, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications.

Our institution is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU). We focus on holistic development of students through academic excellence, practical training, and extracurricular activities.

Vision

To be a leading institution of higher learning, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and producing competent professionals who contribute to society and industry.

Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching methods, industry-oriented curriculum, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, enabling students to achieve their career goals.
